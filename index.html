Arpanet

It was initially created by US government during the cold war, it was used to communicate to various sites without the needs of a direct connection. At the end of the cold war the project ARPA was closed and became MILNET. MILNET was slowly abandoned and in 1990 it was sold to an university where it became the internet that we know.
